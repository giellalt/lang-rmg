# Proper noun inflection
The Traveller Romani language proper nouns inflect in the same cases as regular
nouns, but with a colon (':') as separator.

**LEXICON prop-fem**
**LEXICON prop-mal**
**LEXICON prop-plc**
**LEXICON prop-obj**
